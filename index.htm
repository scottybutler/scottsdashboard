It’s with a heavy heart that we let you know Organic Angels is closing its doors.

We’ve poured everything into keeping the business going — and we genuinely thought we could make it through. But like so many small businesses, the last couple of years have been incredibly tough. With ongoing cost-of-living pressures and rising operational costs, it simply became too difficult to keep things sustainable.

After 19 years of delivering fresh, organic goodness to homes across Melbourne, this has been one of the hardest decisions we’ve ever had to make.

To each of you who supported us — whether you’ve been with us for years or only recently joined — thank you. You’ve helped us build something truly special, and we’re deeply grateful.
